About me

Hi, there! My name is Alina and I live in Brașov. I currently work in a bank, but I have recently started to learn programming, and it's simply AWESOME!!!! I've tried this before in highschool and didn't go too well... Apparently, everything deserves a second chance. :) And so does programming.

I really enjoy going back to school again, even if it is an informal one. It helped me learn a lot of new things and it definitely got me out of my comfort zone.

You can find next to this section a list of my colleagues in the course and their awesome blogs.

Hobbies

Definitely, my favourite activity on earth is running. It doesn't matter if I run all by myself, with friends, or with my 8 years' old son and our dog, it's simply the best way to clear my mind.

Running also helps me stay in shape and is one of the cheapest methods to burn some calories. All only need is a pair of running shoes and a smile. After that... your legs will start moving by themselves. All you have to do is go out and ENJOY THE RUN!

Remember: A good run and a good laugh are the two best cures for anything! :-)

News

So, as I already mentioned before, I've recently took up some programming courses. At first, it all started as an ambition, just to prove myself that I can do this. But it soon became a passion when I realized that there are so many things that can be done with a single line of code.

Of course, the enthusiasm, the patience and the knowledge of my mentors at Școala Informală de IT also helped me with this.

My first lesson: anybody can code!
